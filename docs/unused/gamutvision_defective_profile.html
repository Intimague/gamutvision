Introduction

A beta version of Gamutvision is available here for registered Imatest users.
There will be many refinements prior to the "final" release.

Gamutvision can simulate and analyze the performance of printer/paper/ICC profile combinations from ICC profiles only: you can learn a lot about the performance of a printer/paper combination without having the printer or paper in your possession. All you need is the ICC profile, which can often be dowloaded from the internet. This enables you to do a number of remarkable things.

  • You can get a good idea about what to expect from a printer or paper before you buy.
  • You can determine the quality of ICC profiles, and you can learn which rendering intents work best.
  • You can find out if your printer is performing as specified— by comparing test prints with Gamutvision simulations. It won't tell you why your prints aren't coming out correct— whether it's your printer hardware or software settings, but it will show you exactly what's happening. This can be a huge help in tracing problems.

You can test ICC profiles to see if they are defective. This may not seem like will be able to without the physical presence of the printer or paper. without is its analysis of ICC profile performance, which enables it to diagnose bad profiles.
